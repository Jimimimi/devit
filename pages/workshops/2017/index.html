DEVit 2017 Workshops

Sunday, 21 May

An all day workshop is an exclusive, low participants count, class. You will get 8 hours of intense, focused training on the state of the art technologies of the web today.

  • All day Workshop Ticket price: 250 €
  • Limited to 25 seats / workshop.
  • Schedule: 11:00 AM \- 8:00 PM. Registration will start at 10:00 AM.
  • Coffee and drinks during the day, lunch will be all provided.
  • Please note that a computer will not be provided, so please bring your own laptop to the workshop.
